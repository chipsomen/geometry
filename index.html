Rectangle:

  • Four vertices and four sides
  • Opposite sides are congruent
  • Opposite sides are parallel
  • All four angles are right angles
  • Diagonals are equal; they do not bisect each other
  • Consecutive angles add up to 180°
  • Opposite angles are congruent

Use the sliders to change the side lengths of the rectangle!
No matter what you do, it will always retain these seven properties!

Square:

  • Four vertices and four sides
  • Opposite sides are congruent
  • Opposite sides are parallel
  • All four sides are equal
  • Diagonals are congruent; they also bisect each other perpendicularly
  • All four angles are right angles
  • Consecutive angles add up to 180°
  • Diagonals bisect angle pairs

Use the sliders to change the side lengths of the square!
No mater what you do, it will always retain these eight properties!

Rhombus:

  • All four sides are congruent
  • Opposite sides are parallel
  • Diagonals are NOT congruent
  • Diagonals bisect each other perpendicularly
  • Consecutive angles add up to 180°
  • Diagonals bisect angle pairs

Use the sliders to change the side lengths and angles of the rhombus!
No matter what you do, it will always retain these six properties!

Parallelogram:

  • Opposite sides are congruent
  • Opposite sides are parallel
  • Diagonals are NOT congruent
  • Diagonals bisect each other
  • Consecutive angles add up to 180°
  • Opposite angles are congruent
  • Diagonals bisect angle pairs

Use the sliders to change the side lengths and angles of the rhombus!
No matter what you do, it will always retain these seven properties!
